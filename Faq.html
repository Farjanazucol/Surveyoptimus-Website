FAQ's

Find answers to some frequently asked questions here.

Here you will find answers to the most common questions about SurveyOptimus and how our survey panel works

SurveyOptimus is an online research community created to allow members to influence and help shape the brands they love. SurveyOptimus will present you with opportunities to voice your opinions in surveys and polls from global brands in a unique and transparent insight marketplace. We will take strict measures to ensure the privacy of your data and will only provide information to clients in an anonymized and aggregated form. Your data will never be used for direct marketing and advertising.

By joining our community you are automatically eligible to participate in our surveys. If you have still not joined, please register and make your opinions heard.

You will earn SurveyOptimus points for registering in our community, completing your profile questions, participating in polls and completing surveys. Each point you earn is accumulated and is redeemable after you reach 2500 points in your account. 2500 points has a value of â‚¹500 you can redeem in the form of vouchers or cash in the form of PayPal.

SurveyOptimus Panel will send you e-mail invitations to participate in surveys, notifications to complete profile, and notices about your membership.

To get started you will need to fill out a short signup form on the homepage of our website and click "submit". Then, check your email and confirm that you have subscribed to surveyoptimus.com by clicking on the link provided in the email that we will send you. It's very simple.

We will always respect your decision to leave the community or comeback at your discretion. You can opt-out or deactivate your Account any time you like by just clicking the delete account option in your Dashboard page. Unfortunately, you will loose all the points and any other profile information immediately as we do not keep any information once you deactivate your account. So, if you wish to come back to the community, then you will need to re-register and build your profile again.

1. Login to your account.

2. GOTO "My Account".

3. Click on "Manage Account".

4. Check the checkbox under "Deactivate Account".

5. Click "Submit".

You may unsubscribe from SurveyOptimus Panel at any time. Log in to your panel account and simply follow the steps to unsubscribe and we will stop sending you any future e-mails immediately.

1. Login to your account.

2. GOTO "My Account".

3. Click on "Manage Account".

4. Click on unsubscribe button under "Email Opt Out".

5. Share your feedback.

6. Click "Yes, Unsubscribe" button.

In activity of 90 days or more will result in account and points change to an inactive status. Along the way SurveyOptimus will send reminder email messages at the 30 day and 60 day period. The email message will alert you that inactivity within your account and inactivity on survey starts will deactivate your membership when inactivity reaches 90 days. SurveyOptimus will also send a final reminder email 1 week before the 90 day inactivity period is achieved. Prior to the 90 day period you can reactivate your account and points by sending us an email at support@surveyoptimus.com and allow up to 7 days for us to reactive your points. At the 90 day period 2 things will occur. Your total earned points will be forfeit and your account will be disabled but not deleted. All disabled accounts will have the opportunity to reactive account by sending us an email at support@surveyoptimus.com and allow up to 7 days for us to reactivate your membership status with us.
