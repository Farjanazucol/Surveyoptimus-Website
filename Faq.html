Here you will find answers to the most common questions about SurveyOptimus and how our survey panel works

SurveyOptimus is an online research community created to allow members to influence and help shape the brands they love. SurveyOptimus will present you with opportunities to voice your opinions in surveys and polls from global brands in a unique and transparent insight marketplace. We will take strict measures to ensure the privacy of your data and will only provide information to clients in an anonymized and aggregated form. Your data will never be used for direct marketing and advertising.
